What we bake

Yeast breads

Soft, familiar, everyday-friendly

Yeast breads are mixed, proofed, and baked for dependable texture and flavor. They are great for sandwiches, toast, and daily meals.

Sourdough breads

Slow-fermented and naturally leavened

Sourdough is shaped by longer fermentation windows. Crumb, flavor, and crust depend on timing and temperature, so batch availability can shift.

How ordering works

  1. Review breads and choose what fits your week.
  2. Reach out early to preorder, especially for sourdough timing.
  3. Receive confirmation based on bake schedule and capacity.

Special orders are limited and approved case-by-case under Mississippi cottage food laws.
