Special orders

Limited custom requests

Golden Gulf Bakery accepts limited special orders for yeast and sourdough breads. Requests are reviewed against bake capacity and Mississippi cottage food law requirements.

Approval is required. Ingredient and quantity limits may apply.

Before you request

  • Preorder early for sourdough timing and fermentation windows.
  • Special orders are not guaranteed until confirmed.
  • Availability may vary by bake schedule.
